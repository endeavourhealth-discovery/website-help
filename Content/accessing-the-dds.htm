Accessing the Discovery Data Service

The side menu options that are displayed vary depending on your user permissions and those of your assigned project.

Contact info@discoverydataservice.org if you do not have a username and password.

Sign in to the Discovery Data Service

  1. Navigate to the required DDS app URL.

    The Sign in dialog box is displayed.

    Sign in dialog box

  2. Provide your username and password, and then click Sign in.

    You are then prompted to provide a one-time code.

    One-time code dialog box

  3. Copy the one-time code from your authentication device and then click Sign in.

Accessing Data Sharing Manager for the first time...
If you are accessing the Discovery Data Service for the first time, you need to enable two-factor authentication using the Google Authenticator app.
Download the free app from the App Store or Google Play.
Follow the instructions on screen to install the app on your phone, or mobile device, and create a 6-digit code.
